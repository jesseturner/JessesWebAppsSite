Weather API

Weather predictions from the National Weather Service. All data is from the NWS API (more information: https://weather-gov.github.io/api/general-faqs). My application gets your current location from your browser (if it isn't working - this likely needs to be enabled), then passes your latitude and longitude to the NWS to determine your location's grid ID. After that call is completed, a second call gets the weather predictions for your area.
